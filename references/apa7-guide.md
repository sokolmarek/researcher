# APA 7th Edition: Quick Reference

Loaded by citation-management and journal-formatting skills.

## In-Text Citations

### Parenthetical
- One author: (Smith, 2023)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- Three+ authors: (Smith et al., 2023)
- Multiple works: (Jones, 2022; Smith, 2023), listed in alphabetical order
- Same author, same year: (Smith, 2023a, 2023b)
- Direct quote: (Smith, 2023, p. 45)
- Organization first use: (World Health Organization [WHO], 2023), then (WHO, 2023)

### Narrative
- Smith (2023) found that...
- Smith and Jones (2023) demonstrated...
- Smith et al. (2023) reported...

## Reference List Format

### Journal Article
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical*, *volume*(issue), page–page. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Book
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital of subtitle* (Edition). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Book Chapter
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In E. E. Editor (Ed.), *Title of book* (pp. xx–xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Conference Paper
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Days). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of Conference Name* (pp. xx–xx). Publisher. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Preprint
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of manuscript. *Name of Archive*. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Thesis/Dissertation
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of dissertation* [Doctoral dissertation, University Name]. Database Name.

### Website
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. https://url

## Key Rules

- Italicize journal names, book titles, volume numbers
- Use DOI as hyperlink when available (https://doi.org/xxxxx format)
- Up to 20 authors: list all; 21+: list first 19, ellipsis, last author
- Use "&" before last author in reference list; "and" in narrative citations
- Hanging indent: 0.5 inches
- Double-spaced
- Alphabetical order by first author surname
- No access dates for stable content (journals, books)

## Numbers and Statistics
- Use numerals for 10 and above; words for below 10
- Exception: always use numerals with units (3 cm, 5 days)
- Report exact p values (p = .032), not thresholds (p < .05) when possible
- Italicize statistical symbols: *M*, *SD*, *t*, *F*, *p*, *d*, *r*

## Headings (5 levels)

1. **Level 1:** Centered, Bold, Title Case
2. **Level 2:** Left-Aligned, Bold, Title Case
3. **Level 3:** Left-Aligned, Bold Italic, Title Case
4. **Level 4:** Indented, Bold, Title Case, ending with period. Text continues...
5. **Level 5:** Indented, Bold Italic, Title Case, ending with period. Text continues...
