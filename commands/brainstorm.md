# /brainstorm

Socratic research design refinement.

## Parameters
- **mode** (select: quick/deep/devils-advocate, default: deep): Brainstorming intensity

## Form Fields
- **idea** (text, required): Your research idea, question, or topic -- even if vague
- **mode** (select: quick/deep/devils-advocate, default: deep): How thorough

## Behavior
1. Routes to brainstorming skill with the provided idea and mode
2. In deep mode: initiates multi-round Socratic dialogue
3. In quick mode: rapid 5-question refinement
4. In devils-advocate mode: aggressively challenges every assumption
5. Outputs refined research question, hypotheses, and next steps
6. Saves session to manuscript/brainstorm-log.md
