Researcher is a Claude Code and Cowork plugin with **28 specialized skills**, **9 agents**, and **11 slash commands** covering the whole research pipeline: from a vague Monday hunch to a submission-ready manuscript. It will not do your research for you, and it will not fabricate a single citation. It will handle the brutal logistics so you can sleep.

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Discover" icon="magnifier">
    Multi-source literature search, gap analysis, SOTA tracking, and fact-checking, grounded in real, resolvable citations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Write" icon="pencil">
    Scaffold a manuscript, draft sections in your own voice, and keep every claim tied to a source you can verify.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Review" icon="approve-check">
    A five-persona peer-review panel, revision handling, and point-by-point response-to-reviewers documents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Publish" icon="rocket">
    Figures in TikZ and matplotlib, journal-grade tables, journal and conference finding, and LaTeX or Word output.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

:::note[Honest about what this is]
Researcher is an assistant, not an author. It has no judgment of its own, so every claim it drafts is yours to verify. What it guarantees is the opposite of most late-night writing sessions: no invented data, no fabricated references, and no em dashes.
:::
